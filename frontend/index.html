Business Insights

Data-driven findings and actionable retention strategies for your team

📋

Contract Type is King

Month-to-month customers churn at ~42% versus 2-year contract holders at just 2%. Migrating 10% of M2M customers to annual plans could cut churn by ~15%.

⚡

Fiber Optic Paradox

Fiber users pay more but churn more. They expect premium quality — any service degradation triggers departure. SLA guarantees and proactive communication are critical.

🕐

The First Year is Critical

Churn risk peaks in months 1–12. A structured 90-day onboarding programme with dedicated support can reduce early-tenure churn by up to 25%.

💳

Electronic Check Risk

Customers paying by electronic check churn 30% more than auto-pay users. Incentivising credit card auto-pay with small monthly discounts reduces churn and payment friction.

🛡️

Security Add-Ons Retain

Customers with Online Security enabled churn 18% less. Bundle security and tech support into base plans — the marginal cost far outweighs a lost customer's LTV.

👴

Senior Citizen Segment

Senior customers (16% of base) churn at elevated rates, often due to support difficulties. A dedicated senior helpline and simplified billing reduces this significantly.

🤖 Why Random Forest?

Random Forest was selected as the best model because it handles non-linear relationships, is robust to outliers, provides native feature importance, and consistently outperforms Logistic Regression on imbalanced telecom churn datasets. It ensembles 200 decision trees, reducing variance without sacrificing bias — making it ideal for production deployment where false negatives (missed churners) carry real revenue cost.
